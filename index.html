About

Anne Schuchman is a writer and translator with an MFA in Creative Nonfiction and Literary Translation from Fairleigh Dickinson University and a PhD in Italian Studies from New York University.

Her translations have been published or are forthcoming in World Literature Today, The Southern Review, The Los Angeles Review, Ilanot Review, The Journal of Italian Translation, and The Queens Review.

Her nonfiction work has been published in The Massachusetts Review, Apricity Press, and The Saranac Review, among others.

Awards include a Fulbright Fellowship, an NEH Summer Stipend, and a New Jersey State Council on the Arts grant.
